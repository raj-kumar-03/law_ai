This Professional Services Agreement outlines the terms between the Santa Cruz County Regional Transportation Commission (Commission) and a Consultant for a specified project.  The Consultant agrees to perform services detailed in Exhibit A (Scope of Services), using key personnel (specified in the agreement) who cannot be replaced without the Commission's consent.  Compensation, not exceeding a stated amount, is detailed in Exhibit B (Fee Schedule) and includes progress payments based on satisfactory work and allowable costs.  The agreement includes clauses for early termination (by either party under specific conditions), indemnification, insurance requirements, non-discrimination, and dispute resolution procedures.  The Consultant is classified as an independent contractor, and the Commission retains rights to all work products.  The agreement is subject to Commission board approval and includes provisions for record retention, safety, and subcontracting.
